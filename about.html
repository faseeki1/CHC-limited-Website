Our Profile

CHC is a Property and Development Company promoted by institutional investors and prominent individuals with a core mission to provide affordable housing and real estate investment opportunities to all Nigerians. A member of Real Estate Developers Association of Nigeria (REDAN). We provide creative housing solutions for Upper Middle class citizens nationwide using various models that will deliver affordable housing to its target market. Our company seeks out investment property with latent value, and makes it available for its wide customer base. We pride ourselves with the ability to make real estate investment affordable for any investor. CHC Limited is a profit-oriented organization operating as a holding company through which projects that require individual funding are initiated. CHC as a holding company will deliver returns to its promoters and project investors through revenue generated across multiple stages of property development. We draw on resources and capabilities across the sector to ensure impact in areas within and outside of focus. We secure funding from the capital market, equity investors and regulated organizations and create a commercially driven vehicle in response to market demand with the intent of having a significant impact on home ownership levels in Nigeria. Through accessing an increasing mortgage market and facilitating the development of high quality housing at optimal price points, CHC provides upper middle class the opportunity to own homes directly for the first time. We are focused on the development of 100,000 residential properties by year 2020 acting as a catalyst targeting arrears across the housing value chain. Delivery of the proposed number of houses would have a halo effect across the real estate ecosystem impacting FDI, jobs, capacity building, home ownership and mortgages. Scale •CHC is proposing the most ambitious development of housing to be undertaken in Nigeria to date. •Construction of 100,000 homes over five years should enable the realisation of economies of scale. Affordability •CHC is targeting middle of the pyramid customer base which will enable previously subserviced Nigerians access to affordable housing for the first time. Advocacy •CHC's relationships with fund managers, government, developers, and PMIs can significantly reduce operational risk. •Advocacy will accelerate securitization of land and title, housing uptake and bespoke mortgage product solution. The Real Estate ecosystem and diverse range customer drivers requires CHC to adopt a flexible approach to validating the viability of projects on an individual basis. CHC will need to establish a mechanism to accelerate the development of the business case for each project through the analysis of the variables cited below. Further analysis will be required to secure a deeper understanding of customers within each state. Opportunity vs Prioritisation CHC will target customers located in states with high economic activity, high density and spending power such as Lagos, Abuja, Kano, Rivers, Oyo, Abia, Cross River and Ogun. Developments will be pursued as opportunities to secure land are realised.

Our Board

Alhaji Ali Magashi - Chairman

Ali Mohammed Magashi is a graduate of Aeronautical Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering, from Nigerian College of Aviation Technology, Zaria. An Avionics Engineer, a Flight Engineer, and a former Instructor and National Examiner on the Boeing-727 aircraft. He has attended executive programs in Corporate Strategy and Strategic Finance at Harvard Business School, International Housing Finance at Wharton School and Fannie Mae Institute, in the United States of America.

Hassan Usman - Director

A Chartered Accountant, Hassan possesses a highly relevant, award-winning career spanning over eighteen years in Finance and Investment Advisory and Privatisation Services, covering various sectors and global regions. He brings a deep knowledge in creating various financing solutions to broaden ASO's offering. Prior to becoming CEO of ASO, Hassan served as Executive Director (Investments) at Abuja Investment and Property Development Company Ltd., the Federal Capital Territory's premier development agency.

Elijah Onyeagba - Managing Director/CEO

Elijah is an Economist by training and has an MBA with over a decade experience in commercial, corporate, retail and mortgage banking. He has structured construction finance deals worth over US$150m(NGN30bn) and over US$100m(NGN20bn) in retail, commercial and investment mortgages. Before now, He was the Group Head in charge of Real Estate Transaction in ASO Savings & Loans Plc; Nigeria’s largest originator of mortgage assets with additional responsibility of overseeing the company business concerns within the Federal Capital Territory, North and the Southern Region of Nigeria.
